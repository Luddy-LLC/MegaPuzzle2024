Lore

It watches.

Dragons are interesting creatures. Most notoriously, they are fueled by deep desires, or greed as some call it. The subject of their greed, however, can have variety. Some hunger for knowledge, others for money, and still others for food itself. The great curse of dragons, however, is that they cannot create that which they hunger for most. They cannot create money, they do not know how to develop their own knowledge, and they cannot cook. Because of this, any questions the dragon asks me must have answers that exist elsewhere, it must have taken the words from somewhere. If I have trouble figuring out what answers it seeks, they surely can be found elsewhere...
