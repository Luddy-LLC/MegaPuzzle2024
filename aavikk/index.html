Lore

The land of Aavikk is my primary focus of study. Once a thriving center of learning and magic, it was reduced to ashen rubble when the dragon who lives atop Mount Dyrzai attacked, burningn their cities to the ground and taking or destroying their great libraries.

Little is known about what might have provoked the creature, or why it targetted libraries in taking books, but such is the central thesis of my studies. From what I have found, he much of the information culled in the dragon's fires was about magic, and the people of Aavikk before their destruction seemed to be quite well informed on the mechanics of magic, having mastered the art of portals in particular. They seemed to generally teach that magic flows from ambition, whether that be a positive or negative drive.

The survivors of the dragon's attack ultimately were scattered to the wind, making accounts difficult to come by. However, it is said that those who are more in tune with magic often live much longer lives, and since Aavikk was home to several powerful wizards many speculate some of them settled down in homes far or distant after the attack, given they survived. This, however, is difficult to determine since the people from Aavikk visually looked very similar to the people of Et'wixt, and after so long cast into foreign lands would likely have assimilated.

For all of my work, much is still unknown about Aavikk and its people. It is, however, likely that my efforts to study the cause of their downfall have invoked the same rage of that ancient dragon who took my Cat from me in its attempt to again obfuscate the knowledge of those arts it went through such pains to rid this land of before.
