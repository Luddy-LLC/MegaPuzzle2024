Puzzle Prompt

The Yiv script was used by a small group of English immigrants, Yivlicans, before their decedents fully assimilated into the culture and language of the Ithkiyaani people. The name Yiv came from the earliest known Yiv inscription, shown above. Scholars believe Yiv was likely the name of someone's spouse or significant other who was Ithkiyaani, as the word Yiv does not have a translation in the English language. Yiv is an English-conforming-adaptation of the script for the Ithkiyaani people's language, Ithkiyaaqui. The pioneering document that began widespread study of Yiv and the Yivlicans was the Yivlican Rosetta Stone, shown below:

Dear Evren,
Though these end times near, pray thee return to me on the day when the sun rises anew.
My heart is thine.
