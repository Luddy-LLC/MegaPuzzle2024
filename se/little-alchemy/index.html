A Little Bit of Alchemy

LORE STUFFS, AT MALLORY AND REECE
Hopefully you're able to find a site that makes cooking the dish as straightforward as possible.

  1. Put water and air into a small mixing bowl and lightly stir.
  2. Add a scoop of earth to the bowl and combine.
  3. To a large mixing bowl add equal parts earth and water.
  4. Empty the contents of the small mixing bowl into the larger bowl. Let chill.
  5. To a food processor add fire and air. Set aside half of the mixture in a small pot.
  6. Take the chilled mixture from the fridge and add it to the food processor. Pulse on and off for 30 seconds.
  7. Add earth to the food processor again, and blend until smooth.
  8. Empty the contents of the food processor into the small pot, and blend over low heat.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
