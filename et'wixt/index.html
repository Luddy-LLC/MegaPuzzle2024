Lore

Et'Wixt is one of the more ambitious players on the current world stage, insisting on a ceaseless expansionism which decimates those towns and villages with sits nearest to them. I am, admittedly, rather biased in these affairs since I hail from Ithkiya, which is Et'Wixt's primary rival in terms of power, but I would still like to think of myself as somewhat capable of analasis on these terms. I find myself first and foremost disliking Et'Wixt's tendency to not just conquer but also destroy the culture and livelihood of those which they deem "lesser."

Not only do I disagree that we other peoples are "lesser" than those who live in Et'Wixt, but it hurts my heart knowing how much knowledge and life has been lost in the pursuit of assimilating the rest of known society into their fold. I've dedicated my professional career to recovering information about peoples who were lost, so watching cultures be actively wiped out by Et'Wixt's nationalist tendencies makes me sick when I think of it. I've sometimes personally been accused of being "stuck in the past," but I feel strongly that when histories are erased its hard to know what might lie waiting in our future.
